The Best Movies according to Vidhya

My Top Movies of All Time

Chavva

Chhaava' depicts the aftermath of Shivaji's death, focusing on his son Sambhaji's leadership against Mughal Emperor Aurangzeb's forces. Despite its historical significance, the film has received mixed reviews, with some critics highlighting its lack of depth and overemphasis on action sequences

The wild robots

This animated feature from DreamWorks Animation tells the story of Roz, a service robot voiced by Lupita Nyong'o, who becomes shipwrecked on an uninhabited island. As she adapts to her new environment, Roz forms unexpected bonds with the local wildlife, exploring themes of nature, technology, and motherhood.

Anora

Directed by Sean Baker, 'Anora' follows Ani Mikheeva, a Brooklyn exotic dancer portrayed by Mikey Madison, who becomes entangled in a whirlwind week of romance and danger after a sudden marriage to a wealthy Russian. The film masterfully blends genres, offering a fresh take on modern class exploitation.
